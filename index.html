Who I Am

About Me

I'm a fourth-year student in the B.Tech + M.Tech Dual Degree programme at IIT Bhubaneswar, specialising in System Design within the School of Mechanical Sciences.

My academic training centres on building rigorous, first-principles understanding of mechanical systems — from fluid dynamics and continuum mechanics to machine kinematics and structural analysis. My primary research interest is the intersection of physical engineering and intelligent systems: where hardware meets computation.

I'm actively expanding into machine learning and scientific computing in Python, with a long-term goal of applying data-driven methods to robotics, smart actuator design, and autonomous physical systems.

Project 01 · Ongoing
Ionic Wind Generator (EHD Thruster)

Designing a solid-state electrohydrodynamic (EHD) thruster to generate directed airflow with no moving parts. Successfully identified and integrated a high-voltage DC source to drive corona discharge, and currently iterating on electrode geometry to optimize thrust. Investigating boundary layer control effects for potential drag-reduction applications.

Fluid Dynamics Hardware Electrostatics
Tools: HV Electronics · Prototype Fabrication
Project 02 · e-Yantra IIT Bombay
Krishi Drone Precision Agriculture UAV

Contributed to the development of an autonomous quadrotor for pest and disease detection using overhead camera-based computer vision. The system architecture is built on ROS 2, featuring a navigation stack that handles precision waypoint following guided by real-time image feedback.

Robotics Computer Vision UAV Control
Tools: ROS 2 · Gazebo · Python · YOLO
Project 03 · Simulation
CFD Analysis of IC Engine Piston Heads

Performed reactive-flow CFD simulations in ANSYS Forte to compare combustion performance of Flat-top and Circular Dish piston geometries in a CI engine. Configured RANS RNG k-epsilon turbulence model with CHEMKIN-Pro chemistry solver to confirm superior air-fuel mixing in dish-type geometry.

Thermodynamics CFD Combustion
Tools: ANSYS Forte · CHEMKIN-Pro
Project 04 · Kinematics
2DOF Robotic Manipulator Kinematics

Designed a CAD model of a 2-DOF planar robotic arm using Simscape Multibody and exported its Rigid Body Tree to implement Forward Kinematics. Implemented Inverse Kinematics using the iterative Levenberg-Marquardt solver to trace geometric waypoints and generated smooth Cartesian paths.

Kinematics Trajectory Planning Robotics
Tools: MATLAB Simulink · Simscape Multibody
